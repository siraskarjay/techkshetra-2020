TechKshetra is a technical as well as non-technical event organised by the students of POLYTECHNIC, THE MAHARAJA SAYAJIRAO UNIVERSITY OF BARODA. TECHKSHETRA comprises of two words, one is Tech which means Technology and other word is Kshetra which means field, ultimately, the meaning of TechKshetra is the field of technology, where students get an opportunity to exhibit their technical as well as non-technical skills. Techkshetra is organized since last four years by the students and supportive hands of Polytehnic, The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda. This event accord you platform to unveil your prodigious skills at National Level and give opportunity to intensify your versatility. In 2018, Col.(Retd) Vinod Falnikar had a guest of appearance at our inaugural ceremony to electrify the young aspirants. The aim of this event is to develop healthy competitive spirit among students, promote concepts of innovation, creativity and leadership for benefit of our society.We are continuing the legacy of our event for last three years and adding in the glory of our university. Our aim is to reach each and every state of our nation and manifest the eminence of our university.
