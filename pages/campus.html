    CAMPUS AMBASSADOR

  • Home > Campus Ambassador



We are introducing a designation of "Campus Ambassador" in our event.This program gives you an opportunity to be a part of organising team of national level techfest Techkshetra organised by The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda.



First of all Techshetra is a national level techfest of The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda. Every year more than 6000 students from entire nation take part in our event. Techkshetra is a combination of technical, non-technical, sport events along with workshops, seminars and lots of more interesting programs. Every year we visit many colleges for registration and because of being unable to visit each and every college we are introducing Campus Ambassador Programme where a student can become our Ambassador in his/her college and represent Techkshetra on our behalf.

HOW?

  • To become the ambassador you will have to fill the Campus Ambassador Form and the eligibility criteria for being a Campus ambassador is to have minimum 50 registration from your college done by your own.

  • Registration form will have a column where the participants will fill your unique reference code (The Unique reference code will be provided to you by Team Techkshetra) at the time when you will register.

Benefits

  • Every ambassador of college will be offered The Maharaja Sayajirao University certified appreciation letter.

  • When two or more ambassadors will be from same college then the ambassador who has done maximum registration from his/her reference will get appreciation letter along with goodies and coupons from our sponsors and appreciation trophy.

  • Winning ambassador among all ambassadors of different college will be offered appreciation certificate, discount coupons , vouchers , goodies ,a 4 digit Cash Prize and many more things.
