About Techkshetra


Welcome to a national level event - TECHKSHETRA organized by the students of the THE MAHARAJA SAYAJIRAO UNIVERSITY OF BARODA.Every year we gather a total participation of 6000+ participants in different types of events, seminar and workshop.


Techkshetra is organized since last four years by the students and supportive hands of Polytehnic, The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda. This event accord you platform to unveil your prodigious skills at National Level and give opportunity to intensify your versatility.
